Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you treat?

Every 3–4 weeks through the season—typically six visits. This cadence helps interrupt the mosquito life cycle and maintains coverage.

Is it safe for kids and pets?

We use targeted applications and follow label directions. We’ll advise re-entry timing (often after dry time) based on weather and product.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. Seasonal programs are simple and transparent. You can also request one-time event sprays.

What if it rains?

Weather is factored into our scheduling and product selection. If heavy rain affects a treatment window, we’ll reschedule promptly.
