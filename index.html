Why mosquitoes matter

Mosquitoes aren’t just a nuisance — they can spread illnesses such as West Nile virus and other mosquito-borne diseases. Ticks can transmit Lyme disease and other infections. Reducing mosquito and tick populations around your home lowers the risk of bites and helps protect your family and pets.

By targeting the shaded, humid areas where mosquitoes and ticks rest and breed, our treatments break up their life cycle and help make your property a safer, more enjoyable place to be.
