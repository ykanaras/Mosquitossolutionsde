Our mosquito control program is designed to provide consistent protection throughout the season. With six visits during peak mosquito activity, we ensure your yard stays comfortable and pest free.

Why mosquitoes matter

Mosquitoes aren’t just a nuisance — they can spread illnesses such as West Nile virus and Eastern Equine Encephalitis in the U.S. Reducing populations around your home lowers bite exposure and helps protect your family and pets. Our targeted applications focus on the shaded, moist areas where mosquitoes rest during the day so evenings are calmer and more enjoyable.

We operate our own apiary!

Running an apiary gives us firsthand appreciation for pollinators and healthy ecosystems. We apply EPA-registered products at the lowest effective rates and follow best practices to avoid harming beneficial insects — protecting pollinators is always a priority.
