Why mosquitoes matter

Mosquitoes aren’t just annoying—they’re one of the most common disease vectors. In the U.S., they can transmit West Nile virus, Eastern Equine Encephalitis, and La Crosse virus. While risk varies by season and region, bites are preventable and exposure can be reduced around your home.

Our scheduled program interrupts the mosquito life cycle and reduces daytime resting spots.

  • Source reduction: identify and remove standing water where mosquitoes breed.
  • Targeted applications: treat foliage and shaded harborage zones where adults rest.
  • Consistent coverage: visits every 3–4 weeks through peak season.
