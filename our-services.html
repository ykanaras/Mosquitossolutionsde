Mosquito & Tick Control Services

Reliable protection with treatments every 3–4 weeks — typically 6 visits during the season.

What is the service?

Our mosquito control service targets and reduces mosquito populations around your home with scheduled treatments every 3–4 weeks. We treat high-risk areas in the landscape and around your property where mosquitoes rest and breed—helping you get your evenings back.

At Mosquito Solutions, our treatments are tailored to help homeowners enjoy the outdoors. No more ending the night early due to pesky mosquitoes when you're out at the family barbecue.

Seasonal Program

Six scheduled visits during peak mosquito season help keep populations down from spring through fall. You’ll receive reminders and friendly service every step of the way.

Targeted Treatment

We focus on shrubs, shaded areas, under decks, and other likely harborage zones where mosquitoes rest. This maximizes effectiveness while using the lowest effective rates.

Licensed & Insured

Our knowledgeable team is trained in best practices for effective and safe mosquito, flea & tick control around your property.

Enhance treatment effectiveness

Mosquito Solutions

Improve results by removing standing water (birdbaths, flowerpot saucers, toys, clogged gutters, etc.). Keep grass and vegetation trimmed to reduce shaded resting spots and help treatments work more effectively.

Flea & Tick Solutions

Keep yards clean and well-maintained: regularly mow, remove leaf litter, and trim back overgrown shrubs. If you have pets, wash their bedding frequently and limit time in tall grass or wooded areas. These simple steps reduce pest activity and complement our program.
