Mosquito & Tick Control Services

Reliable protection with treatments every 3–4 weeks — typically 6 visits during the season. Local, licensed & insured technicians you’ll actually know by name.

What is the service?

We reduce mosquito populations around your home by targeting the places they rest and breed. On each visit we apply a precise treatment to shrubs, shaded vegetation, under decks, fence lines and other harborage zones. The result is a comfortable yard you can enjoy from spring through fall — without ending the night early.

Why mosquitoes matter

In our area, mosquito bites aren’t just annoying — they can spread West Nile virus and Eastern Equine Encephalitis. Reducing populations around your home lowers bite exposure for your family and pets.

What we treat

  • Shrubs, hedges, shaded landscaping and leaf litter
  • Perimeter fence lines, wood piles and under-deck areas
  • Moist, shaded corners where adults rest during the day
  • Standing-water sources (identify & advise removal)

How it works

  1. 1) Fast quote & scheduling

    Most properties can be quoted same-day. We schedule your first visit and send reminders before each service.

  2. 2) Targeted applications

    Every 3–4 weeks we treat high-risk zones. Visits are quick; typical dry time is 30–60 minutes, weather-dependent.

  3. 3) Season-long control

    Consistent treatments (usually six) suppress the population curve so you enjoy evenings outside all season.

Program options

Safety & EPA compliance

We use **EPA-registered** products and apply at the **lowest effective rates**. Kids and pets can typically re-enter treated areas after dry time. As always, follow the guidance on your service ticket and any onsite signage left by your technician.

Quick FAQ

How long does a visit take?

Most services take 15–30 minutes depending on property size. You’ll receive a notification when we’re done.

Is it safe for kids and pets?

Yes — after the labeled dry time. We’ll note any special guidance on your service ticket.

What if it rains?

Light rain after dry time usually doesn’t affect results. For heavy storms near your service, we’ll evaluate and reschedule if needed.
