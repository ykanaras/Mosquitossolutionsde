About Mosquito Solutions

Local. Family-run. Focused on safety and results.

Our story & mission

We’re two brothers who started Mosquito Solutions with a simple mission: help people enjoy their yards without the dangers and nuisance of ticks and mosquitoes.

What began as helping out neighbors quickly grew into a local service built on communication, professionalism, and care for your property. We’re licensed, insured, and trained in best practices that prioritize your family, pets, and the environment.

We use EPA-registered products and targeted applications that focus on where mosquitoes rest — shaded foliage, dense vegetation, and other harborage sites. By treating these areas on a regular schedule during peak season, we keep populations down so you can spend evenings outside again.

Respect for your property

We treat carefully around landscaping and take time to answer questions before and after service.

Clear communication

Appointment reminders, friendly techs, and transparent expectations every step of the way.

Safety-minded approach

Kid & pet considerate after dry time. We always follow label directions and industry best practices.
